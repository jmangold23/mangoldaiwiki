Azure DevOps Services Pricing

Feb 7, 2024
1 min read
azure-devops-pricing devops-services pay-as-you-go pricing-calculator free-trial

Azure DevOps Services pricing helps you scale your DevOps capabilities while controlling your costs. With flexible pay-as-you-go options, you can grow your DevOps practice incrementally as your needs change.

Your Azure DevOps Services bill includes the following elements:

Azure Pipelines

  • Flat rate for Microsoft-hosted parallel jobs and self-hosted parallel jobs
  • No charge for the free tier

Azure Artifacts

  • Tiered pricing based on total artifact storage

Azure Boards, Repos, Test Plans, etc.

  • Per user license fees for Basic, Basic + Test Plans, and Open Source plans

You can mix and match Azure DevOps Services components to get the right capabilities for your team. Start with a few core services and add more as your needs grow.
