Unlock the Power of Azure Migrate: Your Gateway to Seamless Cloud Transformation

Azure Migrate is a comprehensive service that simplifies and streamlines the migration, modernization, and optimization of your IT infrastructure for the cloud. This powerful platform provides a unified experience, a range of assessment and migration tools, and seamless integration with third-party solutions - empowering you to navigate your cloud journey with confidence.

A Unified Migration Platform

At the heart of Azure Migrate lies a single, centralized portal where you can start, run, and track your entire migration to Azure. This consolidated approach allows you to manage the end-to-end process efficiently, from discovery and assessment to migration and modernization.

Diverse Assessment and Migration Tools

Azure Migrate offers a wide range of tools to cater to your unique migration needs. The platform includes the Azure Migrate: Discovery and Assessment tool, which helps you assess the readiness, sizing, and cost implications of migrating your on-premises servers, SQL Servers, and web apps to Azure. The Migration and Modernization tool, on the other hand, facilitates the seamless migration of your VMware VMs, Hyper-V VMs, physical servers, and even workloads from other public clouds.

Additionally, Azure Migrate integrates with a diverse ecosystem of independent software vendor (ISV) offerings, further expanding the scope of supported use cases and capabilities. From Carbonite for server migration to Cloudamize and CloudSphere for assessment, the platform’s extensibility ensures that you have the right tools to address your unique migration needs.

Comprehensive Migration and Modernization Support

Azure Migrate empowers you to not only migrate your servers, databases, and web applications to Azure but also modernize them in the process. The platform helps you assess the readiness of your on-premises resources, plan their migration, and optimize them for the cloud, ensuring a seamless and efficient transition.

Whether you’re migrating VMware VMs, Hyper-V VMs, physical servers, or even workloads from other public clouds, Azure Migrate has you covered. The platform also supports the migration and modernization of databases, web applications, and virtual desktop infrastructure (VDI), providing a comprehensive solution for your cloud transformation journey.

Streamlined Migration Workflow

Azure Migrate’s intuitive interface and guided workflows make the migration process more accessible and less daunting. The platform streamlines the entire journey, from discovery and assessment to actual migration and modernization, helping you navigate the complexities with ease.

By integrating with a wide range of ISV tools, Azure Migrate also simplifies the process of selecting and connecting the right solutions for your specific needs. This flexibility ensures that you can leverage the best-fit tools and capabilities to achieve your cloud transformation goals.

Unlock the Full Potential of Azure

As a native Azure service, Azure Migrate is designed to unlock the full potential of the Azure platform. By seamlessly integrating with other Azure services and tools, the platform empowers you to leverage the latest cloud capabilities, optimize your infrastructure, and drive innovation.

Whether you’re looking to migrate your servers, modernize your databases, or transform your web applications, Azure Migrate is your gateway to a seamless and efficient cloud transformation journey. Unlock the power of the cloud and explore the endless possibilities with Azure Migrate.
